Our Story

Our journey continues as we strive to set new standards in education and inspire the next generation of leaders.

1

Founded in 2001

Siddhartha Academy began with a vision to create a nurturing and innovative learning environment for students in Kathmandu. Our founders believed in holistic education and set out to build a school that inspires excellence.

2

Growth & Innovation

Over the years, we expanded our campus, introduced modern facilities, and adopted new teaching methodologies. Our commitment to student-centered learning has made us a leader in education.

3

A Thriving Community

Today, Siddhartha Academy is home to a vibrant community of students, educators, and alumni. We celebrate diversity, foster creativity, and empower every learner to achieve their dreams.
